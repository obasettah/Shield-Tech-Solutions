About us

ABOUT COMPANY

Shield-Tech Solutions is a technology-driven company dedicated to provide innovative and reliable IT solutions to individuals, businesses, and organizations. The company focuses on delivering top-quality digital services that enhance productivity, strengthen online presence, and ensure data security. Shield-Tech Solutions is committed to helping clients leverage technology for growth and success in an ever-evolving digital world.

LOCATION OF COMPANY

Shield-Tech Solutions is located at #28 Imo Street, Ekpo Abasi, Calabar South, Calabar, Cross River State, Nigeria. The company offers remote and online services mostly, enabling clients from any location to benefit from its innovative tech solutions.

VISION STATEMNT

To become a leading provider of innovative and secure technology solutions in Africa, empowering businesses and individuals with cutting-edge digital tools that drive growth, efficiency, and sustainability.

MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, customized, and affordable technology services that solve real-world problems, enhance user experience, and promote digital transformation among small and medium-scale enterprises.

AIM OF COMPANY

The aim of Shield-Tech Solutions is to bridge the gap between technology and business growth by providing effective IT solutions, ensuring digital security, and equipping individuals with relevant tech skills to thrive in the modern digital economy.

CORE VALUES

Innovation: We embrace creativity and continuous improvement to deliver unique tech solutions.
Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our dealings.
Excellence: We are committed to maintaining high standards in service delivery.
Customer Satisfaction:We prioritize client needs and ensure value-driven results.
Teamwork:We believe in collaboration and shared success.
Security:We prioritize digital safety and data protection in all our solutions.
